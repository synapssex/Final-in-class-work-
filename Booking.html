Broken Screen Studios

About

Broken Screen Studios (BSS) is a Jamaican independent game development studio founded in March 2015 and located at 14 Constant Spring Road, Kingston, Jamaica. The studio operates as a Limited Liability Company (LLC) and consists of 12 passionate artists, writers, programmers, and designers, led by one executive director.

Our mission is to create immersive gaming experiences inspired by Jamaican and Caribbean folklore. We strive to deliver unique combat systems, emotionally rich storytelling, and expansive worlds that highlight the culture, history, and supernatural legends of Jamaica.

In addition to large-scale projects, we also develop mobile puzzle games and top-down role-playing games (RPGs) available on the Google Play Store.

Products & Services

Our flagship title, “Blade of the Rolling Calf”, is a dark Souls-like action RPG set in 1985 Jamaica. The story follows a 30-year-old Jamaican man seeking revenge after his wife and children are killed during the Great Duppy Purge. Players battle supernatural forces rooted in Jamaican folklore through intense combat, strategic gameplay, and narrative-driven exploration.

The game is designed for a mature audience aged 16–40 and explores psychological themes, folklore-based horror, morally complex decisions, and intense action. The expected content rating upon release is PEGI-16.

Beyond our primary releases, Broken Screen Studios produces original soundtracks, digital artwork, downloadable content (DLC), and cosmetic expansions. We maintain active communities on platforms such as Discord and Steam and provide free lore material on our official website. Our long-term vision is to build a connected franchise universe based on Caribbean mythology.
