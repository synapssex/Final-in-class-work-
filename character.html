Story

Name: Kaelen Thorne
Age: 16
Birthday: June 14, 2009

Kaelen Thorne was born in the northern village of Varngrave, the son of the chief

and war-general, Grommen Thorne Red Fist. During the first years of his youth,

his world revolved only around his mother and his baby sister. His father was more

of a legend than a man to him, a name carried on the breath of warriors and the

echoes of war. It was not until Kaelen reached the age of twelve that he truly came

to know the presence of his father. “Kaelen, you have now reached the age of a

man.” It was a snowy day when Kaelen first heard those words. Kaelen did not

understand what his father meant by them, nor could he predict what was about to follow.


“Come on, Kaelen! Now is not the time to buckle.” It was night, and the snow fell hard as both father and son climbed up a rocky mountain.


The temperature up the mountain was different from that in the village; ice crystals

formed in your lungs with every breath, and if you stood too long, your joints in

your body would freeze. Luckily, Kaelen had the proper coverage, but as for his

father, a shirt, pants, and boots, and the lantern he used to pave the way


accompanied him. By the time they reached the peak of the mountain, it was

morning. The view was not like any other. Standing above the clouds, the golden,

> warm, and bright sun on your face, the feeling was like no other, “Cough Cough,”

but the air, thin as a thread. “Now is not the time for sightseeing.” Days pass,

and all they did was train. Hours of push-ups, sit-ups, and striking the air until his

fists bled. From morning to night, he trains relentlessly.


Four years pass. And it's recruitment time. Big, strong, bearded men out in the

forest standing around a campfire as they await the return of Kaelen. He was sent

out to kill and bring the head of a Dreadmaw bear. Every son or daughter of the

chief must complete this challenge. He sits on top of a tree as the beast approaches.

He takes a deep breath. “I'm ready.” In the morning, Groomen opens his tent to

see Kaelen sitting on the 15-foot bear head, sharpening his blade.
